AI chat exporter specifics

The exporter injects a floating toolbar on supported AI chat domains (ChatGPT, Gemini, DeepSeek, Copilot, Perplexity, Claude, and Grok). It only manipulates the page in your browser to let you copy or download a conversation you are already viewing.

  • No remote servers: The extension does not proxy or forward the chat content to any backend.
  • Host permissions: Manifest host permissions are limited to the supported chat domains so the script can run there. They do not grant us access to other sites.
  • Third-party terms apply: Each AI provider (OpenAI, Google, DeepSeek, Microsoft, Perplexity, Anthropic, xAI) keeps ownership of their platforms and has their own privacy policies. Our exporter does not override them.
